About Brew Haven

A passion for the perfect cup.

Brew Haven started in 2008 as a tiny corner stand with one stubborn idea — coffee deserves to be treated as an art form. Today we operate our own micro-roastery, source directly from farmers in 7 countries, and serve over 1,800 cups of joy every single day.

Ethically Sourced

Direct trade relationships with farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil and Kenya.

Roasted Fresh Daily

Every bean we serve was roasted no more than 72 hours before it touched your cup.

Champion Baristas

Our team includes finalists from World Latte Art and Brewers Cup championships.

From bean to cup

Our journey is your flavor

01

Sourcing

We fly twice a year to coffee farms in Ethiopia, Brazil, Colombia and Kenya — meeting growers, cupping micro-lots, and selecting only the top 5%.

02

Roasting

Our 12kg drum roaster is operated daily by certified Q-graders. Each origin gets its own roast curve, captured down to the second.

03

Brewing

From V60 pour-overs to 24-hour cold drips — our baristas master 11 brewing methods. Every drink is calibrated by refractometer.

04

Serving

Served in a space designed by award-winning architects, on ceramics handmade by local artisans. Your cup. Your moment.
